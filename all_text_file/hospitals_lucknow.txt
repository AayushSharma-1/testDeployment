Patients' Reviews:

To provide a comprehensive view of these hospitals, we gathered some patient reviews and sentiments:

SGPGIMS: Patients praise the expertise of doctors and staff, along with the availability of modern medical facilities. However, some mention long waiting times for appointments.

KGMU: Patients appreciate the comprehensive medical services and experienced faculty members. The hospital's cleanliness and infrastructure also receive positive feedback.

RMLIMS: Patients value the patient-centric approach and excellent care received at RMLIMS. However, some mention the need for improved waiting area facilities.

Balrampur Hospital: Patients commend the hospital for its gynecology and obstetrics services, but some suggest the need for more streamlined administrative processes.

Apollomedics: Patients laud the state-of-the-art facilities, skilled medical team, and prompt emergency services at Apollomedics Super Speciality Hospital.

Medanta: Patients praise Medanta's advanced medical treatments and compassionate care provided by the doctors and staff.

Sahara Hospital: Patients appreciate the hospital's commitment to providing quality healthcare and the caring attitude of the medical staff.

Shekhar Hospital: Patients mention the personalized care and attention received at Shekhar Hospital, making them feel well-cared for during their treatment.

Vidhya Hospitals & Trauma Centre: Patients value the hospital's prompt emergency services and the availability of various medical specialties under one roof.

Conclusion:
Both the public and private healthcare systems are well-developed in Lucknow. Private hospitals like Apollomedics and Medanta have cutting-edge facilities and skilled medical staff, while government hospitals like SGPGIMS and KGMU are renowned for their super-specialty services. Although there are differences in the experiences that patients have in different facilities, Lucknow's healthcare system is nonetheless thriving and meets a wide range of medical needs. People are urged to do their homework and get recommendations from reliable sources before selecting a hospital to locate the one that would best meet their needs.
